Four Fun Flavours!

Fresh Green Tea

Relax with authentic, fresh Japanese green tea leaves, steeped to perfection. Drink it in the morning, day, evening, or night, take a step back and enjoy the subtle soothing flavour of tea.

Bold Orange-Grapefruit

With a bold blend of hand picked oranges and grapefruits, this flavour is sure to leave you alert and ready for anything. It's the personal favourite of our owner Nik!

Lychee Breeze

As rejuvinating as a morning breeze, this unique lychee flavour is perfectly balanced between sweetness and freshness. Is it pronounced Ly-chee? Is it Pronounced Lee-chy? We don't know!

Lemon-Lime Twist

Based on the classic 4 fruit flavor, we've come back with a modern, zesty twist made from fresh lemons and limes. This classic inspired flavour is perfect for anybody!
